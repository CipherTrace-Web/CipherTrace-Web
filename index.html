Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CipherTrace

Is my data secure with CipherTrace?
+
Yes, security is our top priority. We use bank-grade encryption (AES-256), secure API connections, and comply with industry standards. Your data is stored in secure data centers with regular security audits and compliance certifications.
What accounts and platforms do you support?
+
We support integration with major trading platforms and exchanges. Our API allows connections to virtually any trading account or platform. Please contact our support team to confirm compatibility with your specific requirements.
How long does it take to get started?
+
You can set up a basic account and start monitoring in less than 5 minutes. More complex setups with custom workflows and multiple integrations typically take 1-2 hours with our support team's assistance.
Do you offer API access for developers?
+
Yes, we provide comprehensive REST and WebSocket APIs for developers. Our API documentation is available to all users, and we offer dedicated developer support for enterprise customers.
What is your support availability?
+
We provide email support 24/7 for all users. Enterprise customers have access to priority support and dedicated account managers. Response times are typically within 2 hours for critical issues.
Can I export my data?
+
Yes, you can export your data in multiple formats (CSV, JSON, PDF) at any time. We provide flexible data export options and do not lock you into our platform. All your data remains yours.
